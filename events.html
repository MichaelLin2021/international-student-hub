Student Events

Find social, cultural and society events for students at UEA.

Why Events Matter

Joining events can help international students meet new people, practise English, explore local culture and feel more connected to university life.

Example Event Types

  • International welcome meetups.
  • Society events and taster sessions.
  • Sports and wellbeing activities.
  • Music, club nights and cultural events.
  • Volunteering and community activities.

Tips for New Students

  • Check event location and ticket requirements before attending.
  • Join societies related to your interests or course.
  • Attend welcome events early in the semester to meet people.
  • Go with a friend if you feel nervous attending alone.
