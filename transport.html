Transport Guide

Getting around Norwich and the UK

Travelling Between UEA and Norwich

UEA is connected to Norwich city centre and the railway station by local bus services. This page helps students choose transport options for everyday travel.

Bus Travel

  • Local buses connect UEA campus with Norwich city centre and the railway station.
  • The Blue Line 25/26 is commonly used by students travelling between campus and the city.
  • Students can check First Bus tickets and student passes.
  • Bus travel is useful for shopping, social events and railway connections.

Student Bus Tickets

First Bus provides UEA student ticket options for Norwich travel. Students who travel frequently may benefit from weekly, termly or annual tickets.

Cycling

  • UEA provides information about cycling routes and storage.
  • Cycling can be a cheaper and flexible option for students living nearby.
  • Students should use lights, locks and helmets for safety.

Train Travel

  • Norwich railway station connects students to London and other UK cities.
  • Students can consider a Railcard for discounted rail travel.
  • Use buses or taxis to travel between UEA and Norwich railway station.

Quick Recommendation

  • Daily commute: bus pass or cycling.
  • Occasional city trips: single or day bus tickets.
  • Travelling outside Norwich: train from Norwich railway station.
