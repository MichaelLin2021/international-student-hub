Bank Account Setup

Step-by-step guide for international students

Steps to Open a Bank Account

  1. Choose a bank (e.g. HSBC, Barclays, Santander)
  2. Prepare documents (passport, student ID, proof of address)
  3. Book an appointment
  4. Activate your account

Required Documents

  • Passport
  • Student ID
  • Proof of address
