Safe behaviour

Before Acting on Visa Information

1

Check the source

Use official UEA or UK government information for decisions about visas or immigration status.

2

Ask early

Contact the support team before deadlines, travel or course changes create pressure.

3

Keep evidence

Save copies of emails, documents and official guidance related to your situation.
