Visa and Immigration Support

Guidance for international students who need visa or immigration help.

When You May Need Support

  • Applying for a Student visa.
  • Understanding visa conditions.
  • Checking right-to-study requirements.
  • Changing course or study details.
  • Understanding work conditions during study.

UEA International Student Support

UEA has dedicated International Student Advisers within the Student Life Team who can provide advice and guidance to international students before, during and after their studies.

Contact: studentlife.international@uea.ac.uk

Important Reminder
