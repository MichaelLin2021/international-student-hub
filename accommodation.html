Practical housing guidance for international students in Norwich.

Accommodation Options for International Students

This page helps international students compare common accommodation choices in Norwich. Students should always check official provider websites before booking.

UEA On-Campus Accommodation

UEA accommodation is recommended for students who want to live close to teaching buildings, campus services and other students.

  • Good for first-year or newly arrived international students.
  • No daily travel cost to campus.
  • Bills such as internet, gas and electricity are usually included.
  • Access to on-site security and maintenance support.

INTO UEA Accommodation

INTO UEA accommodation is suitable for students studying through INTO or those who want strong campus-based support while adapting to university life.

  • Located on or near the UEA campus.
  • Useful for students who are new to the UK.
  • Close to study spaces and university facilities.
  • Better for students who prefer a structured support environment.

Crown Place

Crown Place is private student accommodation in Norwich city centre. It may suit students who prefer city living and easy access to shops, restaurants and social spaces.

  • Located in the heart of Norwich.
  • Offers studios, ensuite rooms and shared apartments.
  • Bills are included according to the provider.
  • Good for students who want city-centre convenience.

Pablo Fanque House

Pablo Fanque House is another private student accommodation option in Norwich. It offers ensuite rooms and studios and may suit students who want modern facilities and shared social spaces.

  • Ensuite and studio room options.
  • Shared kitchen and living areas for some rooms.
  • Study and social spaces available.
  • Located close to Norwich city centre.

What to Check Before Booking

  • Contract length, such as 40, 41, 50 or 51 weeks.
  • Whether bills are included.
  • Deposit amount and refund rules.
  • Distance to UEA and available bus routes.
  • Whether the accommodation is managed by a reliable provider.
  • Room type: shared flat, ensuite, studio or private housing.

Recommended Choice by Situation

  • Want easiest campus access: UEA on-campus accommodation.
  • Want strong support as a new international student: INTO UEA accommodation.
  • Want city centre lifestyle: Crown Place.
  • Want modern private student accommodation: Pablo Fanque House.
