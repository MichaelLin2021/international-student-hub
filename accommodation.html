Private Student Accommodation (City Centre)

City Centre

Crown Place

Located in Norwich city centre with strong social environment.

  • Studios, ensuite rooms and shared flats
  • Gym, cinema, games area and social spaces
  • All bills included
  • ~25 min bus to UEA

How to Choose (Quick Guide)

  • First time in UK → choose UEA on-campus
  • Want social life → Crown Place
  • Want modern facilities → Pablo Fanque House
  • Lower budget → St Crispin’s House

Important Tips

  • Check contract length (usually 40–51 weeks)
  • Confirm bills are included
  • Check distance to UEA and bus routes
  • Never pay before verifying the provider
