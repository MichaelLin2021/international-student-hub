Key Locations for International Students

  • Library: Study space, books, academic support and resources.
  • Students’ Union: Events, societies, advice and student support.
  • UEA Medical Centre: GP registration and healthcare support.
  • Bus Stops: Travel to Norwich city centre and railway station.
  • Sportspark: Gym, sports activities and wellbeing facilities.
  • The Square: Central campus area for meeting friends and finding events.
