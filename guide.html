New Student Guide

Step-by-step support for your first weeks at UEA.

Essential First Steps

Step 1

Complete Registration

  • Check your UEA email.
  • Complete online enrolment.
  • Collect your student ID.
Step 2

Arrange Accommodation

  • Confirm your move-in date.
  • Check rent, deposit and bills.
  • Save your address.
Step 3

Open a Bank Account

  • Prepare ID documents.
  • Compare bank options.
  • Apply online or in branch.
Step 4

Register with a GP

  • Find a local GP.
  • Complete registration.
  • Use NHS 111 for urgent advice.

Useful Tips

  • Join student groups early to meet people.
  • Save important contacts such as UEA support services.
  • Check Blackboard and eVision regularly.
  • Attend student events to build confidence and community.
