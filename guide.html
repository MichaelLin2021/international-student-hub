Arrival roadmap

New Student Guide

A structured first-weeks plan for registration, accommodation, money, healthcare and getting settled at UEA.

Step-by-step

Arrival Timeline

Tasks are grouped by when students are likely to need them, reducing cognitive load during a busy arrival period.

Before travel

Prepare your essentials

Check visa documents, accommodation confirmation, travel plans, university email access and emergency contacts.

Day 1

Arrive and get connected

Move into accommodation, save your UK address, connect to Wi-Fi and check your UEA email for registration instructions.

Week 1

Complete university tasks

Complete online enrolment, collect your student ID, check Blackboard and confirm any right-to-study requirements.

Week 2

Set up everyday life

Open a bank account, register with a GP, learn local bus routes and attend welcome events.

Practical advice

Helpful Habits

  • Check your UEA email every day during arrival week.
  • Save Student Services, accommodation and emergency contact details.
  • Keep digital and paper copies of key documents.
  • Ask official support teams if immigration, health or finance information is unclear.

Design note for evaluation

This page uses short task groups instead of long text blocks because questionnaire feedback suggested that new students prefer clear steps and quick scanning.
